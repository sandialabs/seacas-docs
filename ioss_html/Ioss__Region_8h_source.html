70 /** \brief A grouping entity that contains other grouping entities.
71 *
72 * Maintains a list of NodeBlocks, ElementBlocks, NodeLists, CommLists and Surfaces.
73 * [Similar to the "Composite Pattern" in Design Patterns] All interface to
74 * GroupingEntities is through the Region class; clients of the IO subsystem have no direct
75 * access to the underlying GroupingEntities (other than the Region).
76 */
